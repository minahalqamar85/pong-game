## 🕹️ How to Play

### **Controls:**

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| 🖱️ **Mouse** | Move your paddle (left side) smoothly |
| ⬆️ **Arrow Up** | Move paddle up |
| ⬇️ **Arrow Down** | Move paddle down |
| **SPACE** | Start/Pause the game |

### **Objective:**
- Control the **left paddle** (Player) to bounce the ball back to the computer
- Score points when the ball passes the **computer's paddle** (right side)
- The computer controls the right paddle with AI logic
- Game pauses after each point - press SPACE to continue

### **Gameplay Tips:**
1. **Positioning** - Move your paddle to the center to have better control
2. **Angles** - Hit the ball at different heights on your paddle to change its direction
3. **Timing** - React quickly to the ball's movement
4. **Strategy** - The AI has a deadzone, so you can sometimes outmaneuver it
